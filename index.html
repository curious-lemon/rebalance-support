About Rebalance

Rebalance transforms screen time management into an engaging game. Use productive apps to earn credits, spend them on social media. When credits run out, scroll apps are blocked.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Rebalance work?

Rebalance uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to track your app usage. Use productive apps to earn credits, which are then consumed when using scroll apps. When your credits run out, scroll apps are automatically blocked.

Why do I need to grant Screen Time permission?

Screen Time permission is required to monitor app usage and apply shields when your credits run out. This is an Apple requirement for apps that manage screen time.

Is my data private?

Yes! All data is processed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your usage data. We never see which apps you use.

My credits aren't updating

Ensure Screen Time permission is granted and the app has been set up correctly. Try closing and reopening the app, or check that your productive/scroll apps are selected.

How do I change my earn ratio?

Go to Settings in the app to adjust your earn ratio. This controls how much scroll time you earn for each minute of productive app usage.
